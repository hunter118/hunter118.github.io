Research

The research projects listed below are done in 2020-2021, now I'm working on new projects in Game Theory.

Unsupervised Real-World Image Denoising

  • Proposed a novel method used on unsupervised real-world image denoising.
  • Designed a different neural network structure to modify the algorithm NN+BM3D combining DNNs with MAP approaches.
  • The proposed algorithm can achieve 0.1 improvement on average in PSNR.

AI Human Illusion Replication

  • Completed the challenge of replicating human illsions when seeing optical illusion images successfully, using optic flow method with neural networks, during Telluride Neuromorphic Cognition Engineering Workshop.
  • Tuned the parameters of our model, and designed multiple illusion patterns to demonstrate the effectiveness of the model.
  • Designed a metric to measure the illusion intensity of a certain pattern.

High Definition Cryo-EM 3D Reconstruction

  • Given projection images on different angles of a protein, we want to reconstruct its 3D structure. The projection angles are unknown, and the existing method can only provide rough estimation. In order to get a fine-grained 3D model, finer projection angles are needed.
  • Implemented optimization algorithms on manifolds (SO(3)), in order to derive locally better projection angles.
